Army Commissioned Officer

Islamabad, Pakistan

Job description

Join Pakistan Army as Commissioned Officer and Serve your Nation.

Eligibility

A person is eligible for joining pakistan army if he falls under the following criteria:

  • Age between 17-22 as per March-2024
  • Must be Unmarried
  • Male
  • Weight as per body mass index
  • Citizens of Pakistan and domcile holders of Azad Kashmir and Gilgit Baltistan

Qualifications

  • Minimum 55% marks in FA/FSC and equivalent
  • Minimum 60% marks or above in graduation with 50% marks in FA/FSC are eligible for Pakistan Military Long Course
  • 50% marks in FA/FSC for serving army soliders
